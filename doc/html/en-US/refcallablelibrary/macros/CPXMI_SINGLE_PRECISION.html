CPXMI_SINGLE_PRECISION

1036 Decimal part of coefficient for %s in %s looks like %d/%d in single precision

#define CPXMI_SINGLE_PRECISION 1036
The detected number looks as if it arose from a single precision calculation. This can be a potential source of ill-conditioning or numerical instability in a model.
