CPXMI_CANCEL_TOL

1045 Detected nonzero <= the maximum value of either CPX_PARAM_EPRHS or CPX_PARAM_EPOPT at %s, %s

#define CPXMI_CANCEL_TOL 1045
The nonzero coefficient at the specified constraint and variable is less than or equal to the maximum value of either the parameter feasibility tolerance or the parameter optimality tolerance (included by cplex.h or cplexx.h). This situation can be a potential source of ill-conditioning or numerical instability in a model.
