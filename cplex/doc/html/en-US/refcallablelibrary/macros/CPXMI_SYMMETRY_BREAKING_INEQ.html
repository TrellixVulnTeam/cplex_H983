CPXMI_SYMMETRY_BREAKING_INEQ

1039 Chain of symmetry breaking inequalities of length %lld: (%s, %s, ..., %s)

#define CPXMI_SYMMETRY_BREAKING_INEQ 1039
Modeling assistance detected chains of constraints of the form x1 ≤ x2 ≤ ... ≤ xk, often used as symmetry breaking inequalities. Breaking symmetry in a model by means of those inequalities can result in suboptimal performance in contrast to the more elaborate symmetry handling strategies implemented within the solver itself. Consider simply removing these constraints.
