CPXMI_BIGM_COEF

1040 In %s, %s has a coefficient %g times larger than second largest

#define CPXMI_BIGM_COEF 1040
An indicator constraint models a condition where the value of a binary variable controls the activity of a linear constraint. The linear constraint mentioned in this warning has a binary with a coefficient that is significantly larger than all the other coefficients of the constraint. This may be more efficiently modeled by using an indicator constraint where the binary variable with the largest coefficient is used to imply the rest of the constraint.
