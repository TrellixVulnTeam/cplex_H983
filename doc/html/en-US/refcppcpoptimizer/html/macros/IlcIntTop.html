Macro IlcIntTop

Definition file: ilcp/cpext.h
IlcIntTop
Constant to represent the largest integer.

This constant is the positive integer of largest magnitude representable on a given platform: 2^63-1 on 64-bit platforms and 2^31-1 on 32-bit platforms.
