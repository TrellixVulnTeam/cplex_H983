CPXERR_CAND_NOT_POINT

3025 Candidate is not a point

#define CPXERR_CAND_NOT_POINT 3025
The routine CPXXcallbackgetcandidatepoint and CPXcallbackgetcandidatepoint was called in the context CPX_CALLBACKCONTEXT_CANDIDATE, but the candidate for which the callback was invoked is not a feasible point.
