CPXMI_LB_SMALL_NUM

1004 Detected lower bound <= CPX_MINBOUND at %s

#define CPXMI_LB_SMALL_NUM 1004
The lower bound for the specified variable is less than or equal to CPX_MINBOUND (defined in cplex.h). This can be a potential source of ill-conditioning or numerical instability in a model.
