CPXMI_FRACTION_SCALING

1047 Decimal part of coefficients in %s are fractions and can be scaled with %d/%d

#define CPXMI_FRACTION_SCALING 1047
The constraint coefficients contain fractionals with repeated decimals. This can be a potential source of ill-conditioning or numerical instability in a model. Consider rescaling with the suggested scaling factor.
