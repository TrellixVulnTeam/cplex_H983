CPXMI_WIDE_COEFF_RANGE

1048 Detected constraint with wide range of coefficients. In %s the ratio of largest and smallest (in absolute value) coefficients is %g

#define CPXMI_WIDE_COEFF_RANGE 1048
The coefficients in the specified row span a too wide range. This can be a potential source of ill-conditioning or numerical instability in a model.
