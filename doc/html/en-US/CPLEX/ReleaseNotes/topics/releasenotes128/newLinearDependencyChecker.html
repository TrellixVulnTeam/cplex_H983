Improved linear dependency checker in CPLEX V12.8.0

These release notes highlight a new feature, improved linear dependency checker, in IBM CPLEX V12.8.0.

CPLEX V12.8.0 offers an improved linear dependency checker. Historically, CPLEX applied its linear dependency checker to a limited class of problems. CPLEX V12.8.0 applies the new, improved linear dependency checker to a much greater class of problems: almost all linear programs (LPs). If you were disappointed in the past with CPLEX performance in this respect, the CPLEX development team urges you to take another look at this feature. CPLEX applies this new feature with no effort on your part; that is, no need to invoke the new feature explicitly.
